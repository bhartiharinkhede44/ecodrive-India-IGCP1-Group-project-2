Why We Want to use electronic cars

Environmental Benefits: Electric cars produce zero tailpipe emissions, which helps reduce air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. This can contribute to improving air quality and mitigating climate change.

Reduced Dependence on Fossil Fuels: Electric cars can be powered by a variety of energy sources, including renewable energy like solar and wind power. This reduces the reliance on finite fossil fuels, which are associated with supply and price volatility.
